take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Mask acts as a Physical Barrier to protect you and others from viral and bacterial particulates and also reduces transmission of the virus

learn more

Wash Your Hands

Handwashing with soap removes germs from hands. This helps prevent infections because: People frequently touch their eyes, nose, and mouth without even realizing it.

learn more

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.

learn more

What Are The Main Symptoms

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
